Favourite movies of 2024

My Top Three favourite movies of 2024

CODE 8

"Code 8" is a science fiction thriller set in a world where people with supernatural abilities, known as "powers," are marginalized and exploited. The film follows a young man with extraordinary abilities who resorts to criminal activities to support his family. As he becomes entangled in a world of crime, he discovers a larger conspiracy that threatens to upend society's balance. The movie explores themes of discrimination, family loyalty, and the consequences of unchecked power.

Ambulance

Ambulance" is an action thriller directed by Michael Bay. The story revolves around two brothers who attempt a heist to save their family from financial ruin. However, their plan goes awry, leading to a high-speed chase through the streets of Los Angeles. As they hijack an ambulance to escape, they find themselves in a tense standoff with law enforcement. The film is characterized by Bay's signature style of explosive action sequences and high-octane thrills.

Dune

"Dune" is an epic science fiction film directed by Denis Villeneuve, based on the classic novel by Frank Herbert. Set in a distant future where noble houses control planets and a valuable substance called "spice" is central to interstellar travel and politics, the story follows young nobleman Paul Atreides as his family assumes control of the desert planet Arrakis, the only source of spice. Paul's journey intertwines with prophetic visions, political intrigue, and battles for power as he becomes embroiled in the fate of the universe. Villeneuve's adaptation promises stunning visuals and a faithful interpretation of Herbert's intricate world-building.
